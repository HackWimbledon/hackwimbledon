Goodbye to HackWimbledon, Hello to Makeroni

HackWimbledon as we've known it is going away, but in its place we've created Makeroni. Makeroni is an space for Makers which hosts physical and virtual events. Find out what's on at the Makeroni Meetup then Join us on Discord to chat and check out the makeroni.cc website.
